The project

About us

Italian Bluegrass Campout grew out of a love for bluegrass and the spirit that comes with it: jams as meeting places, where people play together, learn from each other, and grow — as musicians and as people. More than a string of concerts, it's a community: we aim for an open, inclusive atmosphere where someone who's played for decades and someone who just picked up an instrument find the same place in the circle. Beyond the annual Campout, we support regular jam sessions taking root in Tuscany, Emilia-Romagna, and across Italy.
